Computer Science Course

The Computer Science course provides students with a comprehensive understanding of computer systems, software development, algorithms, data structures, and more. Students will learn to design, develop, and analyze complex software solutions to solve real-world problems.

The course is ideal for individuals interested in pursuing careers as software engineers, systems analysts, data scientists, cybersecurity specialists, and more.

Course Requirements:

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Basic understanding of mathematics and logic
  • Proficiency in at least one programming language (preferred)
  • Two Letters of recomendation
  • Personal Statement

Waiting Time for Acceptance: 3-6 weeks
