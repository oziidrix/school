Olaf University Student Organizations

Student Government Association

The Student Government Association (SGA) represents the interests of the student body, organizes events, and advocates for student needs.

Greek Life

Greek Life encompasses various fraternities and sororities on campus, promoting brotherhood/sisterhood, leadership, and philanthropy.

Student Newspaper

The student newspaper provides news, opinions, and features relevant to the campus community, fostering journalism and media skills.

Academic Honor Society

The Academic Honor Society recognizes outstanding academic achievement and promotes excellence in scholarship and leadership.

Cultural Club

The Cultural Club celebrates diversity and cultural awareness through events, performances, and educational activities.

Environmental Club

The Environmental Club promotes sustainability, environmental awareness, and eco-friendly practices on campus and in the community.
