Sociology Course

The Sociology course explores the study of society, social institutions, and human behavior. Students will analyze social issues, cultural diversity, and social change through a sociological lens.

This course is suitable for individuals interested in understanding social phenomena, conducting research, and contributing to social justice and community development initiatives.

Course Requirements:

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Interest in social sciences and research
  • Critical thinking and analytical skills

Waiting Time for Acceptance: 2-4 weeks
