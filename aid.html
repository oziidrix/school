Financial Aid

Scholarships
Our institution offers various scholarships to assist students in financing their education. Scholarships are awarded based on academic achievement, leadership qualities, and extracurricular involvement.
Grants
Grants are another form of financial aid available to eligible students. These funds are typically awarded based on financial need and do not require repayment. Our financial aid office can provide guidance on the application process for grants.
Loans
For students who require additional financial assistance, loans may be available. These loans must be repaid with interest after graduation. Our financial aid advisors can help students explore loan options and understand the terms and conditions.
