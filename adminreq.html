Admissions Requirements

High School Transcript

We require a complete high school transcript demonstrating strong academic performance.

Standardized Test Scores

Submission of SAT or ACT scores is required. Scores should be sent directly from the testing agency.

Letters of Recommendation

Two letters of recommendation from teachers or counselors who can speak to your academic abilities and character.

Personal Statement

A personal statement or essay explaining your reasons for applying and your goals for the future.

Extracurricular Activities

We value involvement in extracurricular activities and community service. Please provide a list of your activities and achievements.
