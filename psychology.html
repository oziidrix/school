Psychology Course

The Psychology course explores the human mind and behavior, covering topics such as cognitive processes, emotional development, social interactions, and mental health disorders.

This course is suitable for individuals interested in understanding human behavior, pursuing careers in counseling, therapy, social work, or further academic study in psychology.

Course Requirements:

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Basic understanding of biology and sociology
  • Interest in human behavior and mental processes
