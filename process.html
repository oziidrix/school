Application Process

1
Application
Submit your application form along with all necessary documents. You can submit your application here
2
Review
Your application will be reviewed by our admissions committee.
3
Interview
Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview.
4
Decision
Receive an admission decision based on your application and interview.
5
Acceptance
Accept our offer and complete any remaining admission requirements.
6
Orientation
Attend orientation sessions to familiarize yourself with the campus and resources.
